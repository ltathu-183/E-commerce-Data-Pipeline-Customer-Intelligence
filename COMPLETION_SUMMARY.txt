## 📋 What Has Been Delivered

### ✅ 7 Core Deliverables

#### 1. **Data Understanding (EDA Notebook)**
📊 `notebooks/1_EDA_schema_exploration.ipynb`
- Schema analysis for all 9 CSV tables
- Data quality checks (missing values, duplicates, inconsistencies)
- Table relationship validation
- 4 key business metrics with calculations
- Clear explanation of star schema grain choice
- **Why it matters**: Recruiter sees you understand data deeply

#### 2. **ETL Pipeline (Production Code)**
⚙️ `src/etl_pipeline.py` (500+ lines)
- **DataExtractor**: Load & validate all CSV files
- **DataCleaner**: Remove duplicates, standardize timestamps, validate ranges
- **DataTransformer**: Feature engineering (total_value, delivery_time, is_delayed)
- **DataLoader**: Save to staging and DWH layers
- Proper logging, error handling, configuration management
- **Why it matters**: Shows you understand production architecture

#### 3. **Star Schema Data Modeling**
🗄️ `src/data_modeling.py` (SQL definitions)
- **Fact Table**: fact_order_items (ITEM-LEVEL grain)
- **Dimensions**: dim_customers, dim_products, dim_time, dim_sellers
- **Aggregates**: agg_customer_metrics, agg_product_metrics, agg_monthly_revenue
- **Views**: v_order_metrics, v_customer_order_history
- Complete with constraints, indexes, and comments
- **Why it matters**: Shows data warehouse design expertise

#### 4. **Advanced SQL Analytics**
🎯 `src/analytics_queries.py` (11 queries)
- **Revenue**: Top customers, monthly trends, AOV analysis
- **Segmentation**: Repeat rate, cohort analysis, RFM base
- **Products**: Top sellers, category performance
- **Operations**: Delivery performance, delayed orders
- **Payments**: Payment method analysis
- All featuring: JOINs, GROUP BY, WINDOW FUNCTIONS, CTEs
- **Why it matters**: Shows real SQL mastery

#### 5. **Customer Segmentation (RFM)**
👥 `notebooks/2_RFM_Segmentation.ipynb`
- RFM metric calculation (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)
- Quartile-based scoring system
- 7-segment classification (Champions, Loyal, At-Risk, Lost, etc.)
- Business strategy for each segment
- Visualizations and export
- **Why it matters**: Shows business thinking + actionable insights

#### 6. **Project Documentation**
📖 **Multiple files**:
- `PROJECT_README.md` - Architecture & design decisions
- `PROJECT_OVERVIEW.md` - Visual overview & talking points
- `IMPLEMENTATION_GUIDE.md` - Step-by-step execution
- `INSIGHTS_AND_RECOMMENDATIONS.md` - Business strategy

#### 7. **Business Insights Report**
💡 `reports/INSIGHTS_AND_RECOMMENDATIONS.md`
- 7 key business insights with data evidence
- Specific, measurable recommendations
- Expected outcomes & implementation timeline
- Connection to business strategy
- **Why it matters**: Shows strategic thinking
